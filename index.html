Projects

Music Buddy ♫

React | Express
Click to visit this app!

This project brings to life an idea I had six years ago. Using React and Express, this application allows musicians to keep track of their repertoire and their performances. Musicians can easily add pieces to the repertoire through a form, and then build out the performances with a multiselector. Future versions will include a calendar with all of the performances, a program format to be printed, and the option to send out email invitations.
